Syntax Showcase

%bass f#2 c#2 e2 f#2

# synth
  @INTRO
    #94 45 ---- ---- ---- ----
  @A
    #94 75 x--- --x- ---- ---- %bass

> INTRO *2 A *8

`%var` declares reusable values.
`# track` starts a lane.
`@SECTION` structures song parts.
`#N` clip lines define instrument, level, pattern, and notes.
`>` sets the arrangement order.
